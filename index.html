About Us

Based in Ereipuvari, Nanguneri (Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu), Sam Enviro Solutions is dedicated to promoting sustainable, organic farming methods that enrich both soil and society. We combine tradition and innovation to bring you authentic, eco-friendly products straight from the heart of nature.

  • Embracing sustainable and circular agricultural practices
  • Women-led entrepreneurship driving rural prosperity
  • Traceable, organic products with community impact

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To build a thriving ecosystem where sustainable organic farming enriches soil health, empowers farmers, and delivers pure, nutrient-rich products that nurture both people and planet for a greener future.

Our Mission

  • Advance chemical-free farming methods that restore and preserve biodiversity and soil vitality.
  • Produce authentic, cold-pressed oils and premium nutraceuticals from carefully cultivated crops like groundnut, sesame, moringa, and papaya leaves.
  • Support and uplift local agricultural communities through fair trade, transparent paddy trading, and knowledge sharing.
  • Promote environmental stewardship by minimizing the ecological footprint and fostering circular resource use.
  • Engage consumers with honest, traceable products that contribute to health and well-being.

Core Values

  • Sustainability
  • Integrity
  • Community
  • Innovation
  • Health
